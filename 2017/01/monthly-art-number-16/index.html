Monthly Art #16

Jan 10, 2017 #art

This is a monthly “newsletter”, containing a list of what I enjoyed the most last month. Whether it was a full length movie, short film, music album, book or even a painting.

Documentary - Before the Flood (2016)

  • https://www.beforetheflood.com/
  • IMDB
  • Rotten Tomatoes
  • Kinopoisk.ru

“Paradise that has been degraded and destroyed. We’re knowingly doing this. I just want to know how far we have gone and is there anything we can do to stop it.”

TED - Laura Vanderkam: How to gain control of your free time

“Time is highly elastic. We cannot make more time. But time will stretch to accommodate what we will put into it.”

“Every minute is my choice.”

Book - Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality

  • http://hpmor.com/
  • http://hpmor.ru/
  • Wikipedia
  • Goodreads

Great book! It’s what you dream of while watching the next “Lord of the Rings”, placing your hand across the face (facepalm) when the main character doing something completely illogical.

Article - Taming the Mammoth: Why You Should Stop Caring What Other People Think

You’ve probably been told that many times, but it won’t hurt to read about this problem again and you will find glorious pictures (as always with waitbutwhy.com).
