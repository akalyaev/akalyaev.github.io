This is a monthly “newsletter”, containing a list of what I enjoyed the most last month. Whether it was a full length movie, short film, music album, book or even a painting.

Movie - Captain Fantastic (2016)

  • Rotten Tomatoes: https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/captain_fantastic/
  • Kinopoisk.ru: https://www.kinopoisk.ru/film/841147/

“What is Coca Cola? - Poisoned water.”

The director is trying to show us how far we have shifted from nature. All our modern world (gadgets, virtual reality, fast food) sometimes leads us astray and creates a veil before our eyes. Tim Urban has a really good article on this - Religion for the Nonreligious (ru_RU).
