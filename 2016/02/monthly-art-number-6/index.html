Monthly Art #6

Feb 10, 2016 #art

This is a monthly “newsletter”, containing a list of what I enjoyed the most last month. Whether it was a full length movie, short film, music album or book or even painting.

There were two moments in my life when I desperately wanted to listen to classical music: after watching a brilliant TED talk by Benjamin Zander and after watching this movie.
